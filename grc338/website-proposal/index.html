Introduction

Delly's

Delly's is a beachside ice cream parlour that offers a variety of unique ice cream flavors. The menu consists of both dairy and non-dairy ice cream options, as well as shaved ice and specialty sundaes.

Target audience

The target audience for this website is tourists or beachgoers in the area. The site will contain the full menu and address of the physical location so users will be able to decide on their order without needing to be in the area.

The main reason why people would visit this site is to see the entire menu before visiting the shop, as well as any of the possible allergens of certain flavors. Another main reason they may want to visit the website is to see what flavors and mix-ins that are in the specialty items on the menu that may not be as clear. The site will also house the contact information and the address of the physical location for customers who want to visit.

At Delly's, we are committed to serving delicious frozen treats that everybody can enjoy.
